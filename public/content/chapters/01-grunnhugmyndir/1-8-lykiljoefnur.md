## Lykiljöfnur

**Eðlismassi:**

$$\text{density} = \frac{\text{mass}}{\text{volume}}$$

**Umbreytingar á hitastigseiningum:**

$$T_{\text{°C}} = \frac{5}{9} \times \left( T_{\text{°F}} - 32 \right)$$

$$T_{\text{°F}} = \left( \frac{9}{5} \times T_{\text{°C}} \right) + 32$$

$$T_{\text{K}} = \text{°C} + 273.15$$

$$T_{\text{°C}} = \text{K} - 273.15$$
